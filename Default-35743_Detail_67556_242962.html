数学系“60周年”系庆系列报告

报告主题：Calderon-Zygmund 算子的变差的 L^2 有界性

报告人：陈艳萍 教授 （北京科技大学）

报告时间：2020年8月4日（周二） 15:00-17:00

参会方式：腾讯 会议

https://meeting.tencent.com/s/tvE9xNsPeYBB

会议ID：921 743 370

主办部门：理学院数学系

报告摘要：In this talk, we shall show the $L^2$-boundedness for the jump functions and variations of Calder\'on-Zygmund singular integral operators. This result should be the first general criteria for the variational inequalities for kernels not necessarily of convolution type. The $L^2$-boundedness assumption that we verified here is also the starting point of the related results on the (sharp) weighted norm inequalities appeared in many recent papers.
